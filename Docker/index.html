Pravin Selvaranjan - Junior DevOps Engineer

Pravin is an IT professional and has spent the last 6+ years as a high-performing officer in London's Metropolitan Police Service. Coming from a background that is synonymous with problem-solving, he decided to dedicate his spare time to studying cloud technologies and discovered it would be a perfect fit for him. Utilising online resources, Pravin began his cloud journey which ultimately resulted in him gaining his AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner certificate. His strengths lie in his ability to communicate with people from all walks of life, instilling trust and confidence within them in moments of need. In his spare time, he is a keen self-taught musician and enjoys all things media. Pravin is eager to put his communication and critical thinking skills to good use, believing that as a DevOps engineer they will assist him in meeting all of his client's needs.
